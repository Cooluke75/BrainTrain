Goal: Predict where the train will end up.

Rule: The train must turn at every intersection, progressing from left to right.

After selection, you can choose to skip animation by clicking anywhere on the track.

You will be rewarded with combos-scores for continuously getting right. And lose all combos if you get one wrong.

Be quick! Don't forget there is a 60-second timer!
